Mob Information

To provide you some information before Camp Moonlight is released, I thought it would be fun to share the current mobs that I have in the game.

After venturing deep into the forest, something bizarre happens. All of the sudden, the grass and trees turn purple. Enemies roam freely and jump towards you.

Slimes

A classic in pixel video games, Slimes are small creatures with good intentions, they are happy to be visited. However, their slimy flesh burns the human skin.

The Shadowman

A shy figure of the shadowman will quickly and quietly move towards any unwanted attention. He likes to stay out of where he can be seen. He used to be a human like you and me, but after an event that forced him out of society, The Shadowman hides his face in a cloak with only his bright green eyes visible

The Lurker

The same purple color as the decay grass, The lurker is a small flying disk that stays in place until awoke. Once it senses your presence, it's beat red eye opens as it lurches towards you.
