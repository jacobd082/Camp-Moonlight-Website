Welcome to Camp Moonlight!

Have fun and explore the deep wonders and mystery of the forest.

This is a small indie game made by just me. I am creating an adventure game with a pixel art style with many storylines and goal choices. I won't share all the information about the storyline, since it is very early in development and I don't want to make any promises I won't fulfill. I will tell you this: The game has a sort of spooky style at some points while also having a fun and exciting vibe. I am using free assets on itch.io to speed up development. I intend on eventually using all of my own art in the game.
